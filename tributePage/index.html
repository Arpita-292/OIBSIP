Biography

Gaur Gopal Das is an Indian monk, motivational speaker, lifestyle coach, and author known worldwide for his inspiring talks and life lessons. A graduate in Electrical Engineering, he worked at Hewlett-Packard before choosing a life of service and joining the ISKCON movement in 1996.

His teachings revolve around happiness, relationships, emotional balance, spirituality, and personal growth. With millions of followers across social media, he has become one of the most influential motivational voices of modern India, touching lives with his humor, wisdom, and transformative insights.
